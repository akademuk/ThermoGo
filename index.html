Mobile diesel heater for extreme conditions.

Introducing our new development – ​​the TPU Thermo Go portable air heater.

Thermo Go is a highly mobile air heating system. Whether it is used in tents, shelters or other structures, it guarantees fast and efficient heating. To perfectly match your individual requirements, the system is available in 2 kW and 4 kW versions and is powered by a high-quality and powerful Webasto air heater (made in Germany).

What really sets the Thermo Go apart is its design, which requires minimal deployment time. For uncompromising mobility in the field, all equipment is housed in a compact, extremely robust and lightweight NATO-standard case. The system has its own independent fuel supply with an integrated 8-liter tank, which ensures a long operating time of up to 30 hours (for the Thermo Go 20 version). In addition, the heater offers flexibility in power supply, operating from a 12 V (DC) or 220 V (AC) network as an option.

Moreover, the high efficiency of the device is supported by a modern control system that significantly reduces both energy consumption and noise levels. Finally, to protect your equipment and ensure absolute operational safety, the Thermo Go 20-40 is equipped with a built-in backup power system. This critically important feature ensures correct and uninterrupted shutdown of the device even in the event of a sudden power outage.
